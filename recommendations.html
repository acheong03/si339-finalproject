Recommendation from us!

For Coffee Machines we reccomend a Nespresso Machine for beginners. It is very easy to start with. No need to worry about the shape or size of the grounds or too much about how much water is being poured in. Just choose the coffee bean that suits your palette and pop it in!

For Milk having a milk frother on hand can be useful for many different drinks. It can be used to quickly and thoroughly mix matcha, create designs in cups using syrup or simply froth milk for quick latte art!

For ingredients, it is good to have simple things to elevate yuour drinks such as vanilla syrup and hazelnut syrups!

Notes

These reccomendations are for beginners to brewing and making your own drinks! The machines and ingredients will differ based on your preferences and experience with using machines to make coffee and cafe drink!!
